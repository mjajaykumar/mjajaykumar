Projects / Research

Study of Liver Function Test in Alcoholic vs Non-Alcoholic Individuals

This research focuses on comparing **Liver Function Test (LFT) parameters** in alcoholic and non-alcoholic individuals. The study evaluates biomarkers such as:

  • ALT (Alanine Aminotransferase)
  • AST (Aspartate Aminotransferase)
  • ALP (Alkaline Phosphatase)
  • Total Bilirubin
  • Albumin

The aim is to understand **liver damage patterns and biochemical differences** between both groups.
